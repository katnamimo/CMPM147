Relation to inspring image: - Color: The sky gradient and underside of clouds was picked with a dropper tool from the inspiration image with the website, "https://redketchup.io/color-picker" - Primitives: The clouds are rendered with clusters of varying`ellipsis` primitives. - Polygons: The background mountain is a single randomly polygon using `vertex` calls. - Composition: Different random seed values lead to differently shaped mountains and differently positioned trees. - Life: The position of trees is animated over time as if to simulate the view from a passing train and the slightly responds to the horizontal motion of the mouse cursor. !
